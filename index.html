How It Works

Three Steps to Get Started

01

Create Your Account

Sign up and choose your role — Volunteer or Student. Complete your profile with languages and availability.

02

Join or Create Groups

Volunteers create language groups with schedules. Students browse and enroll in groups matching their level.

03

Learn & Earn Hours

Attend sessions, check in, and grow together. Volunteers automatically earn verified hours toward their certificates.
